What Are The Rules?

  1. Every Subscriber will receive the same amount of tickets after subscribing.

  2. Every Subscriber will have an equal opportunity to win the active prizes.

  3. Once a Subscriber enters a particular sweepstakes they are unable to withdraw their entry.

  4. Upon max number of entries, a random subscriber will be awarded the prize.

  5. The winning entrant will be announced immediately after the live entries close.
